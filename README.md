# financial-statement-analysis-project
I conducted a financial analysis of Avenue Supermarts Ltd using its standalone Balance Sheet and Income Statement for four years. I calculated key ratios in Excel and built an interactive Power BI dashboard to visualize trends, enhancing insights into profitability, liquidity, and efficiency.

I conducted a comprehensive financial statement analysis of Avenue Supermarts Ltd (DMart) to evaluate its financial performance and position over the past four years. The analysis was based on the company’s standalone Balance Sheets and Income Statements, ensuring a focused assessment of its core operations without the impact of subsidiaries.

The project began with data collection and organization in Microsoft Excel, where I systematically extracted relevant financial figures from annual reports. I manually calculated a wide range of financial ratios to gain insights into different aspects of the company’s performance. These included profitability ratios (such as net profit margin and return on equity), liquidity ratios (current and quick ratios), leverage ratios (debt-to-equity and interest coverage), and efficiency ratios (inventory turnover and asset turnover). This manual approach helped strengthen my understanding of financial concepts and ensured accuracy in interpretation.

After completing the calculations, I used Power BI to transform the data into an interactive and visually engaging report. The dashboard included trend analyses, comparative visuals, and key performance indicators, making it easier to identify patterns and evaluate the company’s financial health over time. The visualization enhanced the clarity of insights and enabled more effective communication of findings.

Overall, this project improved my analytical, financial modeling, and data visualization skills, while providing a deeper understanding of how financial ratios can be used to assess a company’s operational efficiency, profitability, and long-term sustainability.
